Frequently asked questions

Who is PACR built for? ↓

PACR is built for runners who want structure without rigidity and progress without ignoring recovery.

How does the cycle feature work? ↓

You can add cycle data manually or via integrations. PACR adjusts session intensity to hormonal phases.

Which devices are supported? ↓

At launch we focus on Apple Watch, Garmin and Coros, with more device support planned.

Yes. Completed runs, daily check-ins and recovery signals all affect upcoming sessions in real time.
